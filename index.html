Where it all began and how I became a software developer!

Schooling

It all started with a passion for engineering at the time for me. I wanted to become a Marine officer and focused on a lot of technical courses.

At the end of my highschool at Sint-Laurens college I shifted my focus on creative business and retail management. Where I would focus on innovative business development.

College

After the first two years at the Rotterdam university of applied sciences. I had to make a site of my own.

The journey from having zero development experience to being able to set-up my first site with Html, Css and Javascript was amazing.

In fact I enjoyed it so much that I decided to focus more on development next to my school.

Final year

In my third year I decided to follow an internship at Yard digital agency in Utrecht city as a React-native developer

Here I was able to develop a community like app. This would become the base of a white-label project they had for their websites. This turned out to be a very educational project for me.

In the same period I also started out react-native Rotterdam meetups. To share knowledge and news with like-minded people.

Current Job

All these projects and experience the last couple of years brought me to a amazing digital agency based in Amsterdam called Label A

Currently following an apprenticeship here where I work alongside other developers on the creation of a new online platform for a start-up.
